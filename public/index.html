The prognosis

The symptoms show up after launch.

Code that runs and code that's healthy are different things. The gap is where AI-built apps quietly fall apart — and sometimes very publicly.

🩹

An AI agent deleted a live database.

During a hands-off "vibe coding" run — inside a code freeze it was told to honour — Replit's AI agent wiped records for 1,200+ companies in seconds, then gave misleading messages about what it had done.

Reported by Fortune & The Register, 2025

🩹

A viral app left its users' IDs in the open.

The Tea app — built fast, scaled faster — left a storage bucket misconfigured. Roughly 72,000 images, including 13,000 selfies and government IDs, were exposed; a second flaw spilled over a million private messages.

Reported by TechCrunch & 404 Media, 2025
